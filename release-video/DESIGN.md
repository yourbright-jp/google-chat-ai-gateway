## Style Prompt

Fresh light launch announcement for a technical internal product. The video should feel airy, dependable, and quick to understand: white-blue surfaces, Google Chat inspired green and blue, and YourBright's clean teal/blue identity. Use crisp interface-like shapes, soft shadows, gentle arcs, and confident typography. Keep the mood bright rather than cinematic.

## Colors

- Background: `#F6FBFE`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Text: `#173144`
- Muted text: `#607482`
- Google Chat blue: `#1A73E8`
- Google Chat green: `#34A853`
- YourBright teal: `#23A2B1`
- YourBright blue: `#0F9BE4`
- Warm release accent: `#FBBC04`

## Typography

- Primary: `Noto Sans JP`, `Inter`, `Segoe UI`, sans-serif
- Accent numerals and labels: `Poppins`, `Inter`, `Segoe UI`, sans-serif

## Motion

- Airy upward entrances with 0.4-0.7s durations.
- Use soft lateral flow for architecture lines.
- Ambient background elements drift slowly with finite repeats only.
- Transitions should feel like panels sliding through a clean product UI.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use a dark or neon tech style.
- Do not use purple-heavy gradients.
- Do not make the YourBright logo tiny or decorative-only.
- Do not crowd the screen with code snippets.
- Do not use generic stock-photo style imagery.
